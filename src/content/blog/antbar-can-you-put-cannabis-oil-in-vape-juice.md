## Ⅰ. What is cannabis oil?

Cannabis oil is extracted from marijuana, a type of annual upright herbaceous plant. It is usually divided into hemp oil (cannabis oil), hemp seed oil (hemp oil) and hemp seed oil (hemp oil). Hemp seed oil (hemp oil), CBD oil, THC oil, hemp oil (cannabis oil) several kinds. One of them, hemp oil, also known as linseed oil, is a type of industrial hemp oil. It contains two kinds of oil: hemp seed oil and CBD oil. Hemp seed oil is extracted from hemp seeds, while CBD oil is extracted from the stems and leaves of the hemp plant. Both types of hemp oil contain little or no THC.

Cannabis oil, on the other hand, consists mainly of CBD oil and THC oil, which is extracted from the flowers, leaves and stems of the cannabis plant. It has a high THC content, usually above 0.3%. THC (tetrahydrocannabinol), on the other hand, is the harmful component of marijuana, and has similar or even stronger euphoric, hallucinogenic, and narcotic effects than controlled drugs, as well as being more addictive, inexpensive, and stealthy. Smoking this type of substance will cause dizziness, vomiting, mental trance, hallucinogenic reaction, overdose will appear, shock, asphyxiation, and even sudden death. Therefore, cannabis oil with high THC content is prohibited in many countries.

## There are many classifications of CBD oils, which can be broadly categorized as:

Full Spectrum Cannabidiol Oil; Pure CBD Isolate Oil; Broad Spectrum Cannabidiol Oil; Cannabidiol Isolate.

Full Spectrum Cannabidiol Oil: Full Spectrum CBD Oil is extracted from the leaves of the cannabis flower without further purification and isolation, and contains all the CBD components of the plant. Full-spectrum CBD oil has a wide range of medical applications, including the treatment of a variety of diseases and conditions.
2. Pure CBD Isolate Oil:

Pure CBD oil is made by isolating and purifying CBD crystals from the leaves of cannabis flowers, and then dissolving the pure CBD crystals in vegetable oil. It contains no other cannabinoid ingredients, only highly pure CBD. Pure CBD oil has a high degree of relevance and efficacy in medical applications, and is particularly suitable for therapeutic regimens that require high concentrations of CBD.

1. Broad Spectrum Cannabidiol Oil: Broad Spectrum CBD Oil is similar to Full Spectrum CBD Oil, but typically does not contain THC and other highly active cannabinoids. Broad-spectrum CBD oil may not be as widely used in medical applications as full-spectrum CBD oil, but it still offers a variety of benefits.
2. Cannabidiol Isolate:

CBD isolate is made from extremely pure CBD crystals that are free of other cannabis ingredients such as cannabinoids and terpenes. It is a highly pure CBD product of up to 99% purity and is commonly used in pharmaceutical, scientific and specific medical applications.

CBD isolate is highly bioavailable and potent, making it especially useful for treatments that require precise dosing.
