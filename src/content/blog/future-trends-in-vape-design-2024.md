## E-cigarettes are ushering in a new wave of screen integration, larger displays, and smart features.

From the initial small size of 13 *7mm, screens have gradually developed into multiple specifications and sizes, such as 0.96, 1.44, and 1.77 inches. This not only provides users with a richer and more impressive visual experience but also a great user-friendly vaping experience.

This trend of larger screens not only adds a sense of fashion to e-cigarettes but also offers users more intelligent operation options. Users can enjoy the convenience of touch screens, such as swiping and clicking. We can easily adjust the output power, switch between different flavors, and even play games, enhancing entertainment and the functionality of the e-cigarette.

The addition of these smart features undoubtedly brings new vitality into the e-cigarette industry and offers consumers more diverse and personalized choices. It also reflects consumers’ higher demands for product transparency and experience and showcases manufacturers’ efforts to continually innovate and breakthrough in the fierce market competition.

## Growth Potential of Displays in the E-cigarette Market

In the e-cigarette industry, compared to non-screen versions, e-cigarettes with screen displays only slightly increase costs. However, this small cost increment provides a new profit growth for e-cigarette manufacturers. Meanwhile, this innovative design allows users to enjoy a more convenient and intuitive operation experience and offers more diverse choices to meet the personalized needs of different user groups.

Vaped e-cigarettes, as a rising star in the tobacco industry, have been committed to reducing the harm caused by tobacco since their birth. They provide smokers with an alternative to traditional tobacco and assist in quitting smoking. Today, they have sparked a global e-cigarette craze.

However, with the continued development of the e-cigarette market, more industry giants and new birth enterprises are flooding into this field, making this market the Red Sea. In such an environment, how e-cigarette companies can strengthen their competitiveness while maintaining their original intention of smoking cessation and harm reduction has become an important issue.

## Exploring the Sustainability of E-cigarette Companies

**Market Demand ** In the e-cigarette sector, companies should have a keen mind on the needs and preferences of their target market consumers. Through in-depth market research and data analysis to ensure their products precisely meet consumer expectations. **Legal Policies Compliance ** In the global market context, companies should closely follow changes in e-cigarette regulations in various countries. They must ensure that their products always comply with local regulations strictly adhere to industry standards and continually improve product quality and safety. **Technology Innovation Driven ** Companies should continuously invest in research and development for innovation and e-cigarette technology. Applying cutting-edge technology can optimize product design, enhance user experience, and meet consumers’ diverse needs for product quality and flavor. **Brand Building and Market Strategy ** Brand building is a core strategy for business development. Companies should strive to enhance brand awareness and reputation. Through differentiated positioning and marketing strategies to strengthen brand characteristics and expand market influence. Additionally, develop scientific and rational market strategies to be employed in the competition. **Market Competition and Channel Expansion ** In the face of intense market competition, companies should continuously improve their product innovation, production capacity, and management capabilities. Actively expand online and offline channels to form a comprehensive sales system, which can increase product coverage and convenience. **Consumer Education and Health Advocacy ** Companies should prioritize consumer education by promoting public awareness and understanding of e-cigarettes through science-based communication and health advocacy. This can reduce negative public opinion and misunderstandings, emphasizing the health advantages of e-cigarettes and guiding consumers to make healthier choices. **Continuous Innovation and Development ** Companies should maintain keen market insight and innovation capability, continually launching new products and technologies to meet changing market demands and consumer needs. Additionally, keep an eye on global e-cigarette market trends and promptly regulate their strategies and market positioning to ensure stable and sustained development. **Corporate Social Responsibility**

Companies should actively take their corporate social responsibility by collaborating with regulatory agencies to promote the healthy and orderly development of the e-cigarette industry. Focus on environmental protection and employee welfare, positively create greater value for society and achieve sustainable development.

Only by adhering to original intentions, continuous innovation, brand-building strengthening, and actively collaborating with regulatory agencies, can e-cigarette companies maintain an advantageous position in the competitive market environment and achieve long-term development.
